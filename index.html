About Us




  • Who we are?

    In its essence, it is a young society but through its wisdom and aim, it has created a place for itself in the hearts of, not only the students but also the teachers alike. Run by a group of students who carry the fire of change in their hearts and who feel the pain of the society. They nourish the aim by providing indigenous ideas that go a long way in keeping the true essence of CBS alive.

  • What we do?

    Character Building Society, CBS, a society that holds a unique position among the societies of Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and technology, GIKI. That upholds the true spirit of society to help the administration and the students to bridge the gap between them. It also aims to instill the true sense of character by providing such projects and opportunities through which the normal folk could polish their character and be good members of the community.

  • Why we do?

    In a world where we need to analyse our mistakes and then rectify them is what really matters. CBS is a society where it trains an individual by making them indulge in projects which helps them to excel and show their true potential, this exercise of learning and showing off one’s skill in a project-oriented environment benefits and aids to their character in personality building by comprehending the basis of work ethics.

TEDx GIKI

Character building society hosted the second edition of Tedx..

It is to CBS credit to organize a much-awaited event which happened in GIKI after 6 whole years, the TEDxGIKI 2019. This year theme “The Harbinger Of Change” brought the Alumni of GIKI on a single platform where they altruistically shared their way to shape the future with their creative ideas and innovations. TEDxGIKI was a success in terms of participation of GIKI students and the ideas shared by the speakers from various fields.

Mess Table Cloth

Mess Table Cloth Creative Design:

The table cloth provided for tables in the central mess are usually plain white and dull to look at, the table mats are no other than picture of a cold drink company which have been there for many years. CBS decided to design a clothe which will not only be something better to look at but also a source of knowledge as well. The prototype consisted of mathematical equations and physics formulaes, it also had a section for famous quotes of macro influencers, businessmen and entrepreneurs etc. the populace of GIKI appreciated the project and their interest exponentially increased. CBS is working on modifying the table clothe for newer and better addition of knowledge for which information is curated. It will be implying its new ideas soon.

Mess Food Wastage

The daily food wastage in mess was analyzed by Character Bulding Society

Being humans and considering the current financial climate where there is a price for everything and that makes it less affordable for the larger masses when it comes to food and other necessary items to sustain life, it is a great concern for us you realize the amount of food wasted everyday by us. CBS decided to analyze the whole mess food wastage problem and after a series of observations and compilation of daily food wastage data, the average food wasted per day was enough to feed about 100 people. this was a very large number and it was CBS’s goal to lessen it. Hence, taking the high road CBS started an awareness campaign in the central mess, which efficiently reduced it to merely approx 30 people food.
